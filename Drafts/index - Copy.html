Welcome to MyPLS

Welcome to My Personal Learning Space (myPLS).

myPLs is a web-based application that allows RIT life-long learners like students alumni and staff to engage in active learning processes.

myPLS allow professors to build adaptable and engaging lesson plans, Learners to maximize engaging learning experiences and provieds a personalized learning and teaching space with information sources as well as groups.
